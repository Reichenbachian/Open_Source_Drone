All pins are on Arduino Mega 2560

##############################
LS9DS1 -> Arduino Mega
------------------------
Gnd -> Gnd
VCC -> 3.3 V
SDA -> 20 (SDA)
SCL -> 21 (SCL)

##############################
RF24L01 -> Arduino Mega
------------------------
Gnd -> Gnd
VCC -> 3.3 V (Split with LS9DS1)
CE -> 9
CSN -> 53
SCK -> 52
MOSI -> 51
MISO -> 50
